NiceHash Buyer Profitability Chart

This website **estimates** the profitability of buying hashing power on NiceHash.

Calculations are made using my WIP nicehash-calculator rewrite (which uses WhatToMine). Fees are currently not accounted for.

Report bugs or suggest features by making an issue in this repository.

Disclaimer: These results may not be accurate. I am not responsible for any losses that may come as a result of this site.

Yellow coins are the most profitable coin of their algorithm. The ROI column is the column you probably care the most about.
